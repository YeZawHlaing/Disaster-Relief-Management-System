## 📌 Overview

**Disaster Relief Management System (DRMS)** is a backend platform designed to help **Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs)** manage disaster response operations efficiently.

The system digitalizes key relief workflows including:

- Disaster event registration
- Household and beneficiary management
- Needs assessment
- Relief inventory tracking
- Distribution planning and execution
- Volunteer and staff coordination
- Operational reporting

DRMS improves **transparency, accountability, and coordination** during disaster relief activities.

---

## 🏗 Architecture

The system follows a **layered REST architecture**.

### Design Principles

- RESTful API architecture
- Separation of concerns
- DTO-based API responses
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Scalable modular backend
- Secure authentication

## ✨ Core Features

### 🆘 Disaster Management
- Register disaster events
- Track affected areas
- Monitor disaster response activities

### 🏠 Household Registration
- Register affected households
- Maintain beneficiary records
- Track household information

### 📊 Needs Assessment
Record household needs including:

- Food
- Shelter
- Non-Food Items (NFI)
- WASH supplies

### 📦 Relief Inventory Management
- Track relief item stock
- Manage item categories
- Record incoming and outgoing inventory

### 🚚 Distribution Planning
Project Officers can:

- Plan relief operations
- Allocate resources
- Assign staff and volunteers
- Define distribution locations

### 📍 Distribution Tracking
- Record distribution activities
- Track items and quantities delivered
- Prevent duplicate distributions

### 👨‍💼 Staff & Volunteer Management
- Manage staff profiles
- Assign operational tasks
- Track participation in activities

### 📑 Reporting
Generate reports for:

- NGO management
- Operational monitoring
- Donor transparency
